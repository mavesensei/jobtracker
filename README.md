# Job Tracker

Smart job application management platform that keeps your job search organized and on track.

**Track every job application, never miss a follow-up again.** Full-stack platform with intelligent reminders, professional templates, and real-time analytics. Built with Next.js, FastAPI, PostgreSQL & Redis for speed and reliability.

[Try Demo](https://jobtracker-hub.vercel.app)

---

## What We Do

Job Tracker helps professionals manage their job search efficiently. Track applications, get reminders to follow up, and monitor your progress in one place.

**In short:** Apply → Track → Follow-up → Land the job

---

## Key Features

- **Application Management** - Store and organize all your job applications with details like salary, interview dates, and offer information
- **Smart Reminders** - Automatic follow-up notifications after 10 days without response
- **Email Templates** - Pre-built templates for consistent, professional follow-ups
- **Analytics** - See your application stats, monthly progress, and maintain your daily streak
- **Multi-User Support** - Secure accounts with email verification and OAuth login
